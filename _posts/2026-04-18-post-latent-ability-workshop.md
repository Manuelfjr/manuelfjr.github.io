[Latent Ability](https://manuelfjr.github.io/latent-ability-ml/) is a browsable workshop site I prepared for a session at the **University of Bristol**, together with **Professor Ricardo Prudencio**. The material was designed to make the session easier to follow through structured notes, activities, answers, and linked notebooks.

## What the site includes

- A guided path from unsupervised evaluation to Binary IRT and 2PL.
- A dedicated section connecting **Beta4-IRT** and **CLAIRE** to latent-ability-aware evaluation.
- Activity pages and answer pages to support hands-on teaching.
- A cleaner navigation layer than the repository root, making the material easier to use during a live session.

## Why it matters

This workshop site is not just a static companion page. It turns research ideas into a **teaching artifact** that participants can browse during and after the session, which helps bridge theory, implementation, and practical discussion.

- Binary IRT and 2PL
- Beta4-IRT and CLAIRE
