[Iris-CV](https://link.springer.com/chapter/10.1007/978-3-030-91699-2_18) The paper shows the results and comparisons of different computer vision networks and algorithms on our newly created Iris flower image dataset. The main goal is to propose a new toy/benchmark dataset that is in fact more challenging.

Itamar Rocha Filho, João Teixeira, João Wallace Lucena Lins, Telmo Silva Filho, Thaís Ramos, Cecília Silva, Ana Clara Chaves Sousa, Felipe de Sousa, Manuel Ferreira Junior, Thaís Gaudencio do Rêgo, Yuri de Almeida Malheiros
